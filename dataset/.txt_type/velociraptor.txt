Name: Velociraptor
Period: Late Cretaceous (75-71 million years ago)
Diet: Carnivore
Size: 6 feet long and 1.6 feet tall at the hip.
Interesting Facts:
- Much smaller than depicted in movies like Jurassic Park.
- Highly intelligent and likely hunted in packs.
- Had feathers, similar to modern birds.
- possessed a large, curved claw on each foot for slashing prey.
