Name: Tyrannosaurus Rex (T-Rex)
Period: Late Cretaceous (68-66 million years ago)
Diet: Carnivore
Size: Up to 40 feet long and 12 feet tall at the hips.
Interesting Facts:
- Known as the "King of the Tyrant Lizards."
- One of the largest land predators to ever exist.
- Had a bite force of about 8,000 pounds.
- Its arms were tiny compared to its body but surprinsingly strong.
