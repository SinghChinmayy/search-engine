Name: Triceratops
Period: Late Cretaceous (68-66 million years ago)
Diet: Herbivore
Size: 26-30 feet long and 9-10 feet tall.
Interesting Facts:
- Had three distinct horns on its face.
- Possessed a large bony frill around its neck for protection.
- Likely used its horns for defense against predators like T-Rex.
- Its name means "three-horned face."
