Name: Brachiosaurus
Period: Late Jurassic (154-150 million years ago)
Diet: Herbivore
Size: 60-70 feet long and 40 feet tall.
Interesting Facts:
- Had an extremely long neck used to reach tall trees.
- Unlike most dinosaurs, its front legs were longer than its hind legs.
- It was one of the tallest and heaviest land animals to ever live.
- Its name means "arm lizard."
