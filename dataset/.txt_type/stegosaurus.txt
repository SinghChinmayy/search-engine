Name: Stegosaurus
Period: Late Jurassic (155-150 million years ago)
Diet: Herbivore
Size: 26-30 feet long and 14 feet tall.
Interesting Facts:
- Famous for the large, kite-shaped bony plates along its back.
- Had four sharp'spikes on its tail, known as a 'thagomizer'.
- Its brain was roughly the size of a walnut despite its large body.
- Its name means "roof lizard."
